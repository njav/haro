OUR VISION

Become a mainstream provider of sustanable solutions to problems affecting vulnerable persons and groupss in Cameroon by the year 203O.


OUR MISSION

Promote the rights of and provide support to vulnerable groups notably women,children,persons with disabilities,indigenous peoples,internally displaced persons,persons living with HIV/AIDS.

OUR ACTIVITIES

IDP identification

Identification of persons internally displaced as a direct consequence of the crises in the North west & southwest reions of Cameroon

Give Hope to the Hopeless

Provision of Psyco-social and material support to those identified

Awareness for all

Education and sensitization programs in schools,colleges and communities focusing on children's rights,Gender Violence,HIV/AIDS and care

No to Gender Based Violence(GBV)

Organisation of campaigns in communities and social groups against Gender based violence.

Let youths Be Professionals

We also run internship programme designed for young professionals wishing to build a career in any of our areas of intervention.
